Who We Are

Built on Relationships,
Driven by Results

BRIT Sport Group is a global football players agency dedicated to representing and advancing the careers of professional athletes. We work across the entire spectrum of the beautiful game — men's, women's, and youth football — with a commitment to integrity, transparency, and excellence.

"We don't just manage careers — we build them. Every player deserves a partner who sees their full potential."

From contract negotiations and transfer management to career planning and market intelligence, our team leverages deep industry knowledge and cutting-edge technology to deliver unmatched service for our clients.
