🎯 Portfolio Page Upgrade — 10 Suggestions

Thinking like a club scout who just received this link on WhatsApp. What makes me stop scrolling, get excited, and pick up the phone?

  1. 1
    "I'm Interested" CTA — A big, obvious action button in header AND sticky bottom bar. The scout shouldn't have to scroll to find how to respond. One tap → WhatsApp message to your agent.
  2. 2
    Urgency & Key Selling Badges — Scrollable chips right below the hero: "Contract Ends Jun 2026", "EU Passport 🇪🇺", "Mandate ✓", "Age 23 — Peak Window". Creates FOMO and instant value assessment.
  3. 3
    Season Performance Stats — Goals, assists, appearances, minutes, key passes in a visual grid. Scouts need hard numbers at a glance — not just the AI text report.
  4. 4
    Visual Scouting Radar — SVG radar chart showing player attributes (pace, technique, vision, aerial, defending). Instantly communicates the player profile type — no reading required.
  5. 5
    AI Scouting Score — A bold circular score (e.g. 85/100) with breakdown categories. Gives the scout a quick mental anchor before reading the full report. Adds credibility.
  6. 6
    "Why This Player" Quick Pitch — 3-4 bullet points: adaptability, leadership, value-for-money, tactical versatility. The agent's elevator pitch — selling points, not just facts.
  7. 7
    Comparison Snapshot — Quick table comparing this player to 2 similar-profile players at higher-value clubs. Shows the scout what "type" this player is and suggests upside.
  8. 8
    Video Thumbnails Grid — Instead of raw iframes, show a cinematic thumbnail grid with play buttons. First video is featured large. Much more visual impact and faster page load.
  9. 9
    Download PDF / Save to Shortlist — Let the scout save a PDF dossier or bookmark the player. Scouts review 50+ profiles — make yours easy to come back to.
  10. 10
    Sticky Bottom Bar with Player Mini-Card — As the scout scrolls, a persistent bar shows player photo + name + "I'm Interested" button. Never lose the conversion opportunity.
